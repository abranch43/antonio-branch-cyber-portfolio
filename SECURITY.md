# Security Policy

## Public-Safe Repository Boundary

This repository contains only public, sanitized portfolio artifacts for cybersecurity, GRC, compliance documentation, incident response planning, vulnerability management reporting, and BranchOS compliance automation concepts.

No real client data, credentials, protected health information, Controlled Unclassified Information, private Federal Contract Information records, passwords, API keys, private contract information, bank records, VA records, Social Security numbers, tax records, production logs, or confidential evidence should be committed to this repository.

## Reporting Security Concerns

Report security issues, accidental sensitive-data exposure, broken public/private boundaries, misleading compliance claims, or repository-hygiene concerns privately to:

Antonio Branch  
Founder & CEO, A+ Enterprise LLC  
Email: abranch@aplus-enterprise.com

Do not open a public issue containing sensitive details. Include the affected path, a concise description, potential impact, and recommended remediation. Do not attach sensitive records unless a secure transfer method is explicitly provided.

## Public Proof Rules

- Label examples as sample, concept, template, self-assessment support, or verified company record.
- Do not represent a portfolio artifact as proof of third-party certification.
- Add an “as of” date to time-sensitive compliance statements.
- Distinguish CMMC self-assessment/readiness support from C3PAO certification.
- Preserve evidence provenance while keeping private evidence outside the repository.

## Responsible Handling

A+ Enterprise will make a good-faith effort to acknowledge credible reports within 3 business days and assess containment or remediation needs within 7 business days. These are operational targets, not contractual service-level guarantees.
