# SPRS Level 1 Summary

## Summary

A+ Enterprise LLC maintains a CMMC Level 1 self-assessment in SPRS under UID S100053243 for FCI/basic safeguarding alignment under FAR 52.204-21. Level 2 readiness will be evaluated only where CUI or contract-specific requirements apply.

This statement is intended for public, employer-facing, and procurement-safe use. It documents a Level 1 self-assessment posture and does not claim CMMC Level 2 certification, third-party assessment status, or authorization to handle Controlled Unclassified Information (CUI) outside applicable contract requirements and validated system scope.

## Business Context

Antonio Branch is the Founder & CEO of A+ Enterprise LLC, a certified SDVOSB, MBE, and SDVE focused on audit-ready public operations, compliance documentation, CMMC readiness, NIST SP 800-171 control mapping, incident response documentation, vulnerability management reporting, and automation-enabled execution through BranchOS.

The Level 1 self-assessment supports basic safeguarding alignment for Federal Contract Information (FCI). It also provides a foundation for structured documentation, evidence tracking, and readiness review when public-sector opportunities include security or compliance requirements.

## Scope Language

This summary is limited to CMMC Level 1 and FAR 52.204-21 basic safeguarding. It should be read as evidence of a documented self-assessment and readiness process, not as a blanket certification statement.

Appropriate use:

- Describing Level 1 self-assessment entry in SPRS
- Explaining FCI/basic safeguarding alignment
- Supporting employer-facing cybersecurity and GRC portfolio review
- Showing audit-ready documentation discipline

Not appropriate use:

- Claiming CMMC Level 2 certification
- Claiming CUI handling authorization without contract-specific review
- Publishing private assessment evidence or internal system details
- Substituting for legal, contracting, or assessor determination

## FCI and CUI Review Approach

For opportunities involving only FCI, A+ Enterprise LLC reviews applicable FAR 52.204-21 basic safeguarding requirements and maintains public-safe evidence of process alignment.

For opportunities involving CUI, DFARS clauses, CMMC Level 2 requirements, or contract-specific security controls, A+ Enterprise LLC will evaluate the requirement against the contract language, data type, system boundary, evidence expectations, and any required assessment pathway before making readiness or performance claims.
